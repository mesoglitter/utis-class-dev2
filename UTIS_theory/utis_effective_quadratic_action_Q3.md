## 3. Auxiliary-field correction

```text
k**2/(a**2*m_chi**2 + k**2)
```

```text

Q0*(a**2*m_chi**2 - alpha*k**2 + k**2)/(a**2*m_chi**2 + k**2)
```

## 5. Effective gradient coefficient


## 6. Effective mass sector

```text

(M0*(a**2*m_chi**2 + k**2) + a**2*alpha*m_chi**2)/(a**2*m_chi**2 + k**2)
```

## 7. Dispersion relation

```text

(Q0*c_s0*k**2*(a**2*m_chi**2 - alpha*k**2 + k**2)**2 + a**2*(M0*(a**2*m_chi**2 + k**2) + a**2*alpha*m_chi**2)*(a**2*m_chi**2 + k**2))/(Q0*a**2*(a**2*m_chi**2 + k**2)*(a**2*m_chi**2 - alpha*k**2 + k**2))
```

## 8. UV limits

```text
Q_eff(UV) = Q0*(1 - alpha)

c_s_eff²(UV) = c_s0*(1 - alpha)

M_eff²(UV) = M0

omega²(UV) = oo*sign((Q0*alpha**2*c_s0 - 2*Q0*alpha*c_s0 + Q0*c_s0)/(1 - alpha))
```

## 9. IR limits

```text

c_s_eff²(IR) = c_s0

M_eff²(IR) = (M0*a**2*m_chi**2 + a**2*alpha*m_chi**2)/(a**2*m_chi**2)
```
